An introduction...

25th of March 2016

Hello readers! Welcome to my online journal slash blog slash image bank slash brain vomit. In case you are unaware, I am travelling to New York, USA to live and work for at least the next three months. I fgured that while I am away, I should be really documenting what I get up to, so that when I am old and feel the need to relive my younger days, I will have a place to source my stories for my uninterested grandchildren.



To elaborate on why I am going to New York, I have been awarded a scholarship from AUT, my university. The scholarship programme, AUT Internz, links graduating students with companies based in the USA. After a Skype interview with Red Antler, the company saw me as a good fit for their workplace, after which, I was then awarded the scholarship from AUT Internz. Red Antler is a place that build brands for startups that are changing how the world works. They work through many avenues of advertising and digital experiences, and that's where I come in. I will be working as a front-end developer, which basically means I will be in the team of people who code up what a website looks and feels like, making sure it runs smoothly and responsively through devices.
